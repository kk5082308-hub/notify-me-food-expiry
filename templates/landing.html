Features Built for Efficiency

Notify-Me simplifies food management using modern visual helpers and PWA technologies.

OCR Receipt Scanner

Upload a picture of your grocery bill. Our integrated EasyOCR engine extracts items, quantities, and brands automatically to autofill logs.

Barcode Scanner

Scan product barcodes using your mobile camera. We fetch details instantly from the Open Food Facts API to speed up entry.

PWA Push Alerts

Works like a native application! Install the website to your home screen and receive instant warning notifications even when the browser is closed.

Visual Color Codes

Instantly recognize critical products. Color markers shift from Green (safe), Yellow/Orange (due soon) to Red (expired).

Waste Analytics

Track category distributions, safe vs. wasted ratios, and monthly consumption timelines using premium Chart.js animations.

Modern Dark Mode

Easy on your eyes. Full support for customizable light and dark styling tokens that match your system preferences.
